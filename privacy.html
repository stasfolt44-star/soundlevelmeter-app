Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Sound Level Meter ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Sound Level Meter mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

Key Point: Your privacy is our priority. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

Information We Collect

Audio Data

  • The App accesses your device's microphone to measure sound levels in real-time
  • Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded or transmitted
  • We do not have access to any audio content

Measurement Data

  • Sound level measurements (decibel values) are stored locally on your device
  • This data is not transmitted to us or any third parties
  • You can delete this data at any time through the App settings

Purchase Information

  • If you purchase a subscription or lifetime access, the transaction is processed by Apple
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • We only receive confirmation of your purchase status from Apple

How We Use Information

We use information solely to:

  • Provide sound level measurement functionality
  • Save your measurement history on your device
  • Verify subscription status for Pro features

Data Storage

  • All measurement data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is protected using iOS file protection
  • We do not maintain any servers that store your personal data

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not require any personal information to function.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all locally stored measurement data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through Apple
  • Request information about data we may have

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
